Why fix old cords?

  1. I have a huge heart for recycling. I also have limited cash most of the time.
  2. Sometimes, I find that certain things in my house are just sentimental and irreplaceable.
  3. The more things that we can repair in our own homes makes us more self-sufficient and consume less.

If you are looking to learn more, I suggest Bill Marken's book, How to Fix Just About Anything.

The first time I performed this simple fix was with a close friend. Her lamp was an antique. We looked up a video online after following instructions in the book she had on hand. Later on, I was looking at on-line videos and found some helpful tutorials. In the end, we found instructions on how to re-wire her lamp completely! Now it has an antique body but a new age "spirit".
