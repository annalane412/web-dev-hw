Hollandaise Sauce

Ingredients

  • Two eggs (separated)
  • 1/4 Lemon (or ~ 1 tsp)
  • pinch of salt
  • pinch of cayenne (or black pepper)
  • 1/4 cup butter

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients (except butter) in small blender.
  2. Blend ingredients thouroughly.
  3. Melt butter on stove.
  4. Blend egg mixture, and believe me, keep blending.
  5. Do NOT allow the butter to "cook" or "brown". However, it must be HOT before combining with egg mixture.
  6. When butter is at the perfect temperature, ~280-300 ℉, slowly add to egg mixure while blending with whisk.
  7. When butter is fullly combined, blend on high for at least 2 minutes.
  8. Enjoy! Use with steamed asparagus or on eggs benedict.
